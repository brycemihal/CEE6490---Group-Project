     Points of diversion as described on this claim are the points at which the
     stream or natural channels enter claimant`s property.  This right is evidenced
     by Civil Decree #4473 and covers the use of Black Slough waters as they have
     naturally and historically flowed upon the claimant`s property.  Black Slough
     naturally floods these lands during the months of April through June; 92 out
     of 214 days of the irrigation season.  Therefore, the annual diversion limit
     under this right is limited to 92/214 x 548.57 acres x 4.0 AF/acre/year =
     940.4 AF/yr.
